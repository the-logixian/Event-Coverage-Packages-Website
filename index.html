03 — Terms & Policies

How We Work

Clear expectations from day one. Here's what you need to know before we get started.

💳

Payment

  • A 50% deposit is required to confirm and hold your event date. No date is secured without it.
  • The balance is due 48 hours before the event. No crew is dispatched without full payment.
  • All prices are exclusive of 7.5% VAT, which will be reflected on your invoice.
  • Payments via bank transfer only. Account details provided on invoice.
  • Deposits are non-refundable if the event is cancelled within 14 days of the date.
📦

Deliverables

  • All files are delivered via a private online gallery link. Physical handover is not standard.
  • Delivery timelines begin from the day of the event, not from the date of final payment.
  • Raw, unedited footage is not included in any package unless explicitly stated or purchased as an add-on.
  • Revision requests must be submitted within 7 days of receiving the final edit. One round of revisions is included.
  • Download links are active for 30 days. Please download and back up your files promptly.
📅

Bookings & Cancellations

  • Bookings are only confirmed upon receipt of the 50% deposit and a signed service agreement.
  • Date changes must be requested at least 21 days in advance and are subject to availability.
  • Cancellations made more than 14 days before the event may receive a 50% refund of the deposit.
  • CINEmorph Studios reserves the right to use event footage for portfolio and promotional purposes unless a non-disclosure clause is agreed in writing.
🚁

Drone & Special Equipment

  • Drone coverage is subject to venue, airspace, and local authority approval. We handle the necessary checks.
  • If drone operations are not permitted on the day, the equivalent value will be applied to an agreed alternative.
  • LED wall and display screen add-ons require venue confirmation of power availability and access at least 3 days before the event.
📡

Livestream

  • For all livestream packages, a technical site visit is strongly recommended at least 72 hours before the event.
  • CINEmorph Studios is not liable for stream interruptions caused by venue power failure or force majeure events.
  • Zoom credentials and platform access details must be provided at least 24 hours in advance.
  • Private streaming portal setup (Premiere tier) requires a 5-day lead time from booking confirmation.
🤝

Our Commitment

  • We operate on a strict integrity and timeliness policy — if we commit to a date, we show up fully prepared.
  • All crew members are briefed on event protocols and dress appropriately for the occasion.
  • We will always communicate proactively if any unforeseen circumstance affects delivery timelines.
  • For any concerns post-delivery, reach us at hello@cinemorph.studio within 7 days.
